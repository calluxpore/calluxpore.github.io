Hello there!

I’m Sam, I’m a designer and researcher with years of experience across professional practice and academia. These days, I’m deeply focused on designing for and with Artificial Intelligence.

I hold a Master’s in Digital Futures from OCAD University (Canada) and another Master's in Product Design from Coventry University (UK), which gives me a unique lens on how we tackle modern design challenges. My work is all about finding new ways to create knowledge and helping others get comfortable with hands-on computational methods. Right now, I’m specifically looking at how AI and human memory intersect to build the next generation of memory-support tools.
